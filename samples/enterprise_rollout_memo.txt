Enterprise rollout memo

Project: Knowledge Assist pilot
Owner: Digital Workplace team
Target launch: July 15

The pilot will start with the customer support and finance operations teams. The main business goal is to reduce the time employees spend searching policy documents and repeated support notes.

Current risks:
- The highest launch risk is delayed access to approved policy documents.
- A secondary risk is low adoption if reviewers cannot see which source passages were used in each answer.

Action items:
- Maya will confirm the final policy document set by July 3.
- Omar will prepare a short reviewer walkthrough that shows upload, indexing, question answering, and source citations.
- The team will keep human review in place for answers with weak or missing context.

Success measures:
- Support analysts should find relevant policy answers in under two minutes.
- The demo should show the answer, the sources retrieved, and the specific source passages used by the LLM.
